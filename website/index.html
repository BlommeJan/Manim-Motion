03 — Process

From Canvas
to Cinema

01
Place & Style

Drag shapes from the sidebar. Edit fill, stroke, opacity and rotation in the Properties panel. Add LaTeX expressions and coordinate axes.

02
Animate

Create morphs between any two shapes. Add Move, Scale, Fade, or Rotate clips with your choice of 17 easing functions.

03
Preview

Press Space for 60fps real-time playback with sub-frame interpolation. Scrub the timeline ruler to review any moment.

04
Render

One click via Tools → Render HQ. Choose 480p to 4K. Docker compiles your scene to Manim and delivers an MP4.
